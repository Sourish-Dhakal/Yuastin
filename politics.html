December 1st Thursday 2022

South Yuastin Hospital Overflowing

The hospital in South Yuastin has not enough beds because of the new covid variant. It's important that everyone stays safe and physical distances. The hospital won't be accepting anyone who isn't infected with Covid. Don't forget to wear your mask and sanitize your hands at all times. Make sure you're safe. We, in Yuastin, care about your safety but we need to keep our priorities straight and not allow anyone not infected with covid in the hospital to make sure the hospital runs smoothly.

Registration for High Schools Close

If you're applying for high schools in Yuastin, that time window has now closed. If you had applied for an RLCP program they are no longer allowing applications. If you didn't manage to put your application in during the application period, they won't allow you in their school. Better hope you got into the program you wanted to go to or else you go to your homeschool.
