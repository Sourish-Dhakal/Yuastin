Mayor

Sourish Dhakal

Sourish Dhakal is the Mayor of the city of Yuastin. He's 3 years into his run as mayor for this city. His life before being mayor included being an excellent businessman and the CEO of Sour Corporations. His contributions to the city include donating 100 thousands dollars to homeless people who are living on the streets. He's also built a lot of hospitals and recreational centers.
